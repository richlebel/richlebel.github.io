About Me

I began my career as an Electronics Technician and after a fun and rewarding 25 years of service retired as an Engineering Officer responsible for leadiing ship inspection teams and managing conventional and nuclear contracted ship repair. I've been exposed to problems that appear to have no solution and have found nothing is impossible with teamwork, discipline, and hard work.
